Multimedia Film Festival (MMFF) 2024

The 𝗠𝘂𝗹𝘁𝗶𝗺𝗲𝗱𝗶𝗮 𝗙𝗶𝗹𝗺 𝗙𝗲𝘀𝘁𝗶𝘃𝗮𝗹 (𝗠𝗠𝗙𝗙) 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟰 has finally come to an end. "𝗧𝗵𝗲𝘆 𝗦𝗲𝗲," directed by Jerson Tumacder, "𝗟𝗶𝗴𝗵𝘁𝘀 𝗢𝘂𝘁," directed by Kimberly Kho, "𝗠𝘂𝗲𝗿𝘁𝗲,"directed by Kurt Raniel Valencia, and "𝗟𝗮𝗯𝘆𝗿𝗶𝗻𝘁𝗵," directed by Shane Steven Servas, has blown the minds of its viewers at the Audio Visual Room (AVR) inside the PUPQC campus grounds. 𝙈𝙧 𝙇𝙤𝙬𝙚𝙡𝙡 𝘿𝙖𝙫𝙚 𝘼𝙜𝙣𝙞𝙧, a Senior Frontend Developer, and 𝙈𝙧 𝙋𝙚𝙩𝙚𝙧 𝙅𝙤𝙝𝙣 𝙏𝙚𝙣𝙚𝙯𝙖, a software developer, who were both PUPQC Batch 2019 Graduates and former SRG 7th Gen were one of the two judges of the said film showing. Joining them was 𝙈𝙧 𝙅𝙤𝙝𝙣 𝙆𝙮𝙡𝙚 𝘽𝙧𝙮𝙖𝙣 𝘼. 𝘾𝙖𝙢𝙖𝙣𝙞𝙖, an SRG 10th Generation and a Developer and Business Analyst. The former speakers from the Multimedia Seminar last January 26, 𝘿𝙞𝙧𝙚𝙠 𝙂𝙞𝙢𝙨𝙤𝙣 𝘼𝙡𝙚𝙢𝙖𝙣𝙞𝙖 and 𝙈𝙨 𝙍𝙞𝙤 𝙇𝙚𝙜𝙖𝙨𝙥𝙞 were also part of this esteemed line-up.
